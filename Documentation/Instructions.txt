# Portal 3
## Controls
- WASD (Forward, Left, Back, Right respectively) or Arrow Keys (Forward, Left, Back, Right)
- Use mouse to look around

## Goal
- Pass all of the levels (depending on difficulty)
- Pass each level by getting to the green pad through the use of portals and physics

## Rules
- You must use your two portals to get to the necessary places within the level as physics allows.
- The dark walls are disabled to shoot portals onto
